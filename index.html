Below is a canvas where you may place out particles with different charge and study the electric field lines between them.

Click to create new charges and use the controllers below to change the settings.

This simulation is fastest in Google Chrome, although it runs decent in Mozilla Firefox 15 too.

Your browser does not support the HTML5 canvas tag.

Limitations:

  • All field lines only start at positive charges, causing some negative particles to lack lines.
  • The integrator is terrible and will cause the lines to look very ugly at times. Please ignore this and adjust the settings instead.
  • All calculations are approximate and may diverge greatly from what you'd expect.
